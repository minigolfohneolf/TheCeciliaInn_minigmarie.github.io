Inazuma

Enter our Inazuma-inspired room and be whisked away to the mesmerizing and dynamic world of Genshin Impact's Inazuma region. This room is designed with a modern and chic aesthetic, boasting clean lines, minimalist furnishings, and bold accents in stunning shades of blue and gold.

Relax in the comfort of the luxurious queen-sized bed, enveloped in soft, high-thread-count linens and plush pillows. Gaze out of the large windows and take in the breathtaking views of the surrounding cityscape.

Unwind in the cozy seating area and savor a steaming cup of tea or coffee while admiring the sleek and futuristic decor.

Embrace the futuristic charm of Inazuma from the comfort of our B&B with this exquisite, themed room.
