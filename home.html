The Cecilia Inn

Welcome to the cozy and charming B&B located in the heart of the world of Teyvat. Our lovely guesthouse offers comfortable accommodations and a warm atmosphere that will make you feel right at home.

From breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape to convenient in-room amenities, you'll find everything you need to make your stay as enjoyable as possible.

When you're ready to explore the world of Genshin Impact, you'll find plenty to see and do in the surrounding area. From scenic hikes and picturesque villages to thrilling battles and awe-inspiring landscapes, there's something for everyone in this magical world.

After a day of adventure, return to the comfort of our B&B and relax in our cozy lounge or outdoor terrace. Share stories of your travels with fellow adventurers or simply sit back and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.
