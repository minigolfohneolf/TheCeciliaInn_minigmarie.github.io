Sumeru

Enter our Sumeru-themed room and immerse yourself in the world of the wise and scholarly Sumeru region from Genshin Impact. This room is designed with a refined and intellectual aesthetic, featuring ornate furnishings, intricate designs, and a calming palette of cool blues and greens.

Relax in the comfort of the king-sized bed, enveloped in soft, high-quality linens and plump pillows. Take in the serene views of the surrounding mountains and lush gardens from the large windows or curl up with a good book in the cozy armchair.

The room also boasts a seating area where you can enjoy a cup of tea or coffee while appreciating the elegant Sumeru-inspired decor.

Experience the sophistication and wisdom of Sumeru in the comfort of our B&B with this exquisite themed room.
