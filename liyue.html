Liyue

Step into our Liyue themed room and be transported to the exotic and vibrant world of the Liyue region in Genshin Impact.

This room is designed with traditional Liyue aesthetics in mind, featuring richly decorated furnishings, intricate woodwork, and warm, earthy tones.

Relax in the comfort of the plush queen-sized bed, surrounded by luxurious silk pillows and blankets. Take in the stunning views of the surrounding mountains and rivers from the large windows, or cozy up with a good book in the comfortable armchair.

The room also features a small seating area where you can enjoy a cup of tea or coffee while taking in the intricate details of the Liyue-inspired decor.

Experience the magic of Liyue in the comfort of our B&B with this exquisite themed room.
