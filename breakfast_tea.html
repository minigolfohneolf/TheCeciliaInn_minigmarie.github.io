Breakfast & Tea

Start your day off right with our delicious and nutritious breakfast, available from 6:30 to 11 in the morning. Inspired by the vibrant and diverse cuisine of Teyvat, our breakfast offerings include a range of dishes that will tantalize your taste buds and provide the energy you need for a day of adventure.

Whether you prefer sweet or savory, our breakfast options are sure to satisfy. From fluffy pancakes with fresh berries to savory egg dishes with local vegetables, there is something for everyone. And don't forget to try our signature Teyvat-inspired tea, available all day long.

At our B&B, we pride ourselves on providing a unique and memorable experience for our guests, and our breakfast offerings are no exception. Join us for a delicious and satisfying breakfast that will fuel your day in the magical world of Genshin Impact.
